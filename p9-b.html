About the role

Join Google's PM team as a new graduate and help shape the next generation of products used by billions. You'll work cross-functionally with engineering, design, and data science to define product strategy and drive execution.


Responsibilities:

  • Define and prioritize product requirements based on user research
  • Collaborate with engineering and design on feature development
  • Analyze product metrics and identify improvement opportunities
  • Communicate product vision to stakeholders

Qualifications:

  • Bachelor's or Master's degree in a relevant field
  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
  • Excellent communication and leadership abilities
  • Experience with product development methodologies (Agile a plus)
